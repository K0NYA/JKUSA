All of the Student Council Leaders are required to be available for all students and staff at all time weather physically or via phone calls, texts emails etc. But to schedule a sit down in person with a student leader, the best time is from 2PM to 4PM every weekday.

A yearly report is an open document made public to all students summarizing how the Student Council leaders have taken up to different tasks through out an academic year. This is to facilite transparency and allow students to be up to date on current affairs within the council, give insight, compliments and raise any concerns.

Before being concidered for a particular service the student intends to offer, he/she will be vetted with a few credentials to ascertain their ablity to carry out the task for the benefit of the campus.
